Directions to make guacamole

This page is about directions for how to make guacamole.

The Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. (May adjust quanitity of lime and salt to your preferred taste).
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  3. Stir in chopped jalapeno pepper to desired level of spiciness
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
